3rd Fitness Lab

The 3rd Fitness Lab is a one-stop fitness center in Quezon City furnished with advanced equipment and machines, an artificial turf, and a full-sized basketball court (that can be converted into a volleyball court), this sports hub looked like a haven for elite athletes and leagues.

Aside from being a gym and physical center, there are coaching, programs available for strengthening & conditioning exercises or bulking, up properly, rly, people who want to lose weight the right and healthy way and more.
